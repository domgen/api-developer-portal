required
string [ 11 .. 12 ]
Enum: "PREDELIVERY" "POSTDELIVERY" "NOTREQUIRED"

string [ 5 .. 11 ]

  • LETTER:
  • NOTREQUIRED:

retrieves all APPROVED policy updates (cancellations, downgrades and replacements) for a given date range. For example from 2024-01-01-00.00 to 2024-01-02-00.00 would return 24 hours worth of data. Note the date range is based on when the change was approved by Domestic & General as opposed to the transactionDate which is when the update action was originally requested.

Authorizations:
BearerAuth
query Parameters
approvedFromDate
string/([12]\d{3}-(0[1-9]|1[0-2])-(0[1-9]|[12]\d|3[...
Example: approvedFromDate=2026-01-01-00.00

From Date as YYYY-MM-dd-hh.mm (exclusive) - captures all updates that were approved after this date and time

approvedToDate
string/([12]\d{3}-(0[1-9]|1[0-2])-(0[1-9]|[12]\d|3[...
Example: approvedToDate=2026-01-02-00.00

To Date as YYYY-MM-dd-hh.mm (inclusive) - captures all updates that were approved at or before this date and time
